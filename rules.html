AMD Rules

All AMD members are expected to comply with these rules at all times. Breaking the rules may result in a warning, removal from AMD, or a ban from AMD, subject to leaders' discretion.



Adult

  • Must be highly active.
  • Age 18+.
  • Must respond to discord pings/DM's within 24 hours.
  • Be respectful in clan chat and use English.
  • Adult is an FWA clan. If you're not familiar, please ask a leader.
  • Read all clan mails thoroughly and act accordingly.
  • Do not donate any troops to war clan castles.
  • Minimum 100 multiplayer attacks won per league season (minimum 50 by midseason).
  • Minimum 1000 troops donated and 1000 troops recieved per league season.
  • Clan games participation is mandatory (2000 points minimum).
  • If you are going to be inactive for any duration, or if you are going to leave the clan for any reason, let a leader know.
  • Adult does not accept alt accounts as they tend to become inactive. Talk to a leader if you want to put your alt in MOU or Destructor.
  • If you participate in raid weekend, you must use all 6 raid attacks.
  • Do not bring alts into the clan for raid weekend until there is less than 24 hours left.
  • Don’t snipe when someone is attacking a clan capital district.
  • If you start raiding a capital district, you must finish it, unless you run out of attacks.
  • As of Janurary 2024, Adult is a maxed out capital hall 10, so please dump all capital gold in MOU.
  • Do not take your capital gold to non-AMD clans.
  • We do lazy CWL. Please see the Discord for more details.
