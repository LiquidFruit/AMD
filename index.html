Adult

Adult is our flagship FWA clan. We have high requirements and standards in Adult.

  • Minimum 1K donate/recieved in a season.
  • Minimum of 100 attacks per season and 50 at Midseason.
  • Open in-game

MOU

MOU is less active than Adult, but requires near-daily activity.

  • Minimum 300 donate/recieved in a season.
  • Minimum of 30 attacks per season and 15 at Midseason.
  • Open in-game

Sea Eagles

Sea Eagles is our most relaxed FWA clan.

  • Minimum 200 donate/recieved in a season.
  • Minimum of 15 attacks per season and 5 at Midseason.
  • Open in-game

Homeless Club

Homeless Club is active, but has less requirements than Adult or MOU.

  • Minimum 200 donate/recieved in a season.
  • Minimum of 25 attacks per season and 10 at Midseason.
  • Open in-game
