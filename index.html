Welcome to AMD

Welcome to our Clash community! We are an alliance of clans - Adult, MOU, Sea Eagles, and Destructor. Adult, our active flagship, thrives on high participation. MOU strikes a balance with medium activity requirements. Sea Eagles is currently a casual future FWA clan with no requirements. Destructor is primarily used for alts and is not FWA. Join us as we build, clash, and forge bonds in our diverse Clash of Clans family.

Discord (https://discord.gg/yMEpQQkdMC) is required for all AMD members.

Adult

Adult is a very active FWA clan. We have high requirements and standards in Adult.

  • Age 18+.
  • Th12+.
  • Knowledge or willingness to learn FWA wars.
  • 100+ multiplayer attacks won per season. 50 by midseason.
  • 1000 troops donated and 1000 troops received per season.
  • Active daily.
  • Open in-game

MOU

MOU is our more relaxed FWA clan.

  • Age 18+.
  • Th12+.
  • Knowledge or willingness to learn FWA wars.
  • At least 1 multiplayer attack won per season.
  • At least a few troops donated and received per season.
  • Open in-game

Sea Eagles

Sea Eagles is a future-FWA casual clan. There are currently minimal requirements here as we focus on growth and establishing a community.

  • Open in-game

Destructor

Destructor is our 3rd clan and currently isn't FWA. We may make it FWA in future. We do relaxed wars here and are currently just focusing on leveling it up and gaining members.

  • Open in-game
